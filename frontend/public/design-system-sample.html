Rule

If the text was created by the user or identifies a specific entity (project name, task name, product name, agent name), render it in #ffc300. Everything else stays #e1e1e1 or #8f97b7.

Elevation (Dark Theme — Surface Lightening)

In dark themes, elevation is communicated by lightening the surface — not by shadows (which are invisible on dark backgrounds). Higher elevation = lighter surface + optional subtle depth shadow.

Flat (0) #0e1c2d
